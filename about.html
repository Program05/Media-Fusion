About Media Fusion

I built this site out of my passion for anime and video games. I've been playing games for over 22 years, starting with The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, the first game I ever played — and the one that made me fall in love with gaming.

My journey with anime began around the same time, when my dad introduced me to Dragon Ball Z at the age of six. That was over 20 years ago, and I've been hooked ever since.

Today, I turn that lifelong passion into creative work — designing websites, telling digital stories, and bringing interactive projects to life. My goal is to one day create games and digital worlds that inspire others the way those early experiences inspired me.
